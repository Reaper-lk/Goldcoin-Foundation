Frequently Asked Question

What is the Goldcoin?

Goldcoin is an open-source cash payment system that scales on-chain without the need for L2 solutions. It enables instant payments to anyone, anywhere in the world, and can been sent without a transaction fee..

What is the mission of the Goldcoin Foundation?

The Goldcoin Foundation aims to support and promote the adoption of Goldcoin through education, community building, and technological advancements. We work to ensure Goldcoin remains a sustainable and accessible cryptocurrency for everyone.

How can I buy Goldcoin?

You can buy Goldcoin on supported cryptocurrency exchanges. Visit CoinGecko's Goldcoin Page for a list of trusted platforms where Goldcoin is traded.

How do I store Goldcoin?

Goldcoin can be securely stored in the official Goldcoin wallet, which is available for download on the website.
